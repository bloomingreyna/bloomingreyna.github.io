WINDOW CLEANING

We strive to make all of our services as special as possible, but we'd be lying if we said our Window Cleaning didn't hold an extra special place in our areas of expertise. When it comes to window cleaning, we always put in the time and effort needed to make your windows shine.


The condition of your windows is incredibly important to us, which is why we never use any power washing equipment when cleaning them. Such methods are prone to leaving scratches on the glass, so instead, we opt for the much gentler option of cleaning each and every pane by hand.


First, we'll go around your window frame with microfiber cloths to extract any dirt and debris. Then, we'll wet your window with our specialized cleaning solution, give it a thorough scrub to get the stains and grime off, and finish it off with a squeegee. Rinse and repeat until they're all clean!


GUARANTEED:

  • Crystal clear glass
  • Long-lasting shine via specialized cleaning solution
  • 7-day rain warranty

PRICING GUIDE:

Our minimum price varies depending on if you are looking to get your exterior and interior windows cleaned, or just the exteriors. Additionally, we have conditional per pane pricing. The prices are as follows:

  • Exterior + interior windows: $289.99, then $12.00 per pane above 20
  • Exterior windows only: $209.99, then $8.00 per pane above 20

AUTO DETAILING

Instead of going to the car wash, why not just let the car wash come to you? With Weldi's premier Auto Detailing, you can relax while we give your vehicle the deep clean it deserves.


From sedans, to vans, to trailers, we work on vehicles of all shapes and sizes. When you go to a car wash, the equipment and machinery are blind to any distinctions between models, resulting in a vehicle that looks clean at first glance, but certainly doesn't feel clean.


We're different. We go in and clean down to the last detail—inside and outside—right in your driveway, all without the use of any potentially damaging power washing equipment. We also make sure to use professional techniques to make sure that no ugly marks or water stains build up. The result? A vehicle that shines just like it did the first day you bought it.


GUARANTEED:

  • Car that looks good as new
  • Long-lasting shine via specialized cleaning solution
  • Gentle cleaning methods that don't scratch or stain

PRICING GUIDE:

Our prices vary depending on the type/size of vehicle you own. We have six categories of vehicles, which are all priced at the following:

  • Sedans: $150
  • SUVs: $175
  • Vans / 3+ rows of seats: $200
  • Trucks: $250
  • RVs / trailers: $450
  • Commercial vehicles: $1000

If you have any details about your vehicle that you would like us to be aware of, feel free to disclose them during the estimate process.
